Welcome to Illistrin FC

Welcome to the official website of Illistrin Football Club. Our club is proudly rooted in the townland of Illistrin on the outskirts of Letterkenny, Co. Donegal. As a club with deep local ties and a dedication to developing players of all ages and abilities, we are more than just a football team ‚Äî we're a football community.


Illistrin FC continues to go from strength to strength since the club first competed in the inaugural Letterkenny Schoolboys League which was established in October 1976. The club's success is firmly rooted in the foundations laid in those early years by club stalwarts such as Terry Leydon and Tom McGinty, from modest beginnings to teams now competing on the national stage on a regular basis. 2026 will see over 300 players and 20 teams from our club competing at every age group from U7 to Youth League with five U12 teams, five U14 teams and three U16 teams along with 1 Youth team.


Although Illistrin FC prides itself in the development of players at every level - our U12 girls and U14 boys reached the latter stages of the SFAI National Trophy in 2025, keeping Illistrin FC prominent on the national stage. Many of our players, both male and female, have also enjoyed success with Donegal schoolboy and schoolgirl teams and with Finn Harps development squads.
